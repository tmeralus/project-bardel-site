Our mission

" Library Dr Bardel Belizaire is a non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring people of all ages to excel in literacy. Located in the tiny village of Moron (Grand-Anse) in Haiti, the library seeks to open a wealth of knowledge to people in the surrounding communities who do not have access to books or technology. The donation of books and laptops has allowed the library to remain open for one year. YEAH! One year of reading and learning of technology. With donations we can keep the library open for more years to come."
