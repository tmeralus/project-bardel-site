Our mission

" Library Dr Bardel Belizaire is a non-profit organization dedicated to inspire people of all ages to excel in literacy. Located in the tiny village of Moron (Grand-Anse) in Haiti, the library seeks to open a wealth of knowledge to people in the surrounding communities who do not have access to books or technology. The donation of books and laptops has allowed the library to remain open for one year. YEAH! One year of reading and learning of technology. With donations we can keep the library open for more years to come."

Help us reach our goals of building an energy efficient state of the art library
equipped with resources and tools necessary for conducive education and development to succeed.
